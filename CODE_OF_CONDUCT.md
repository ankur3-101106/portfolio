# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone visiting or interacting with this portfolio website. All users, collaborators, recruiters, contributors, and community members are expected to engage professionally and respectfully.

## Expected Behavior

Visitors and contributors are encouraged to:

* Be respectful and considerate in communication.
* Provide constructive feedback.
* Respect differing opinions and experiences.
* Use appropriate and professional language.
* Report inappropriate behavior responsibly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hateful speech.
* Personal attacks, trolling, or intimidation.
* Sharing malicious content, spam, or phishing attempts.
* Unauthorized access attempts, exploitation, or abuse of systems.
* Posting sensitive or private information without permission.
* Any activity that violates applicable laws or ethical standards.

## Cybersecurity & Ethical Use

As this portfolio may include cybersecurity-related projects, demonstrations, or research:

* All showcased tools and techniques are intended strictly for educational and ethical purposes.
* Visitors must not misuse any shared resources, scripts, or concepts for unauthorized or illegal activities.
* Any testing against systems or infrastructure without explicit permission is prohibited.

## Intellectual Property

All original content, including projects, designs, write-ups, and code samples, remains the intellectual property of the website owner unless otherwise stated.

You may:

* View and learn from publicly shared material.
* Reference projects with proper credit.

You may not:

* Re-upload, redistribute, or commercially use content without permission.
* Claim showcased work as your own.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it through the contact information provided on this website.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

* Removal of comments or submissions.
* Blocking access to the website or associated platforms.
* Reporting severe violations to relevant authorities if necessary.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions related to this portfolio website, including:

* Contact forms
* Discussions
* Project collaborations
* Linked platforms and repositories
* Community interactions

## Changes

This Code of Conduct may be updated at any time to reflect improvements, legal requirements, or community standards.
